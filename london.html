Why should you visit

London is, without a doubt, a place that's steeped in history. The city is romanticised in a way few others capitals are. But what if we're still a little mystified about what makes London unique? If we are, then it can be a bit more challenging to rationalise taking the trip to the city – even if we're inundated with information proclaiming just how great it is. Ideally, we want details. And that's what we'll be providing you with in this website.

Culture

London is a friendly city, but it is anonymous and huge. Londoners tend to stick to their own areas, it's just too much of a pain to travel, other than for work. So if you are visiting London, come to the likes of Hackney, Haringey and Lewisham. You may be surprised.
